Cosmetic Surgery Procedures at Dr Anzarut Plastic Surgery

Dr Anzarut Plastic Surgery in British Columbia, Canada specializes in a range of cosmetic surgery procedures for the face, neck, body, and minimally invasive treatments. Each procedure is designed to address specific aesthetic concerns. Below is an overview of the services we offer.


Face & Neck


Brow Lift (Forehead Lift)

A brow lift rejuvenates the forehead area by reducing wrinkles and lifting sagging skin. This procedure can create a more youthful and alert appearance. It targets frown lines and elevates drooping eyebrows. The result is a smoother forehead and a refreshed look.

Facelift Surgery (Rhytidectomy)

Facelift surgery addresses signs of aging in the face and neck. It tightens loose skin and smoothens deep folds. The procedure restores facial contours and reduces sagging. Patients achieve a more youthful and rested appearance.

Neck Lift Surgery, Neck Sculpting (Platysmaplasty)

Neck lift surgery improves the appearance of the neck and jawline. It removes excess skin and fat, and tightens underlying muscles. This procedure helps define the jawline and reduces neck bands. The outcome is a smoother and firmer neck profile.

Eyelid Surgery, Eyelid Lifts (Blepharoplasty)

Eyelid surgery enhances the appearance of the upper and lower eyelids. It corrects drooping lids and removes puffiness. The procedure can improve vision obstructed by sagging eyelids. Patients look more awake and rejuvenated.

Lip Lift Surgery

Lip lift surgery shortens the space between the nose and the upper lip. It enhances the fullness of the upper lip and reveals more teeth when smiling. The procedure creates a more balanced and youthful lip appearance.

Nose Surgery (Rhinoplasty)

Nose surgery reshapes the nose for aesthetic or functional reasons. It can alter the size, shape, and proportions of the nose. The procedure improves facial harmony and can correct breathing issues.

Ear Surgery (Otoplasty)

Ear surgery adjusts the position, shape, or size of the ears. It is often performed to set prominent ears closer to the head. The procedure can enhance facial symmetry and boost self-confidence.

Body Sculpting


Mommy Makeover

A mommy makeover addresses body changes after pregnancy. It combines procedures like breast enhancement and tummy tuck. The goal is to restore pre-pregnancy body contours. Patients regain confidence and feel rejuvenated.

Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty)

A tummy tuck removes excess skin and fat from the abdomen. It also tightens weakened abdominal muscles. The procedure results in a firmer and flatter stomach profile.

Arm Lift (Brachioplasty)

An arm lift reduces excess skin and fat between the underarm and elbow. It reshapes the arm for smoother skin and contours. Patients enjoy more toned and proportionate upper arms.

Thigh Lift (Thighplasty)

A thigh lift reshapes the thighs by reducing excess skin and fat. It improves the appearance of the inner or outer thighs. The procedure results in smoother skin and better-proportioned contours.

Breast Augmentation

Breast augmentation enhances the size and shape of the breasts using implants. It can restore breast volume lost after weight reduction or pregnancy. The procedure enhances balance and improves self-image.

Breast Lift (Mastopexy)

A breast lift raises and firms the breasts by removing excess skin. It tightens surrounding tissue to support a new breast contour. The result is a more youthful and uplifted breast profile.

Liposuction

Liposuction removes excess fat deposits to slim and reshape specific body areas. It targets stubborn fat that doesn't respond to diet and exercise. The procedure enhances body contours and proportion.


Minimally Invasive Treatments


BOTOX

BOTOX injections reduce facial wrinkles and fine lines. They work by relaxing underlying muscles. The treatment is quick and requires no downtime. Results provide a smoother and more youthful appearance.

Chemical Peels

Chemical peels improve skin texture and tone by removing the outer layers. They treat wrinkles, discoloration, and scars. The skin regenerates, revealing a fresher and smoother complexion.

Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers restore lost volume and smooth wrinkles. They can enhance facial contours and plump lips. The treatment offers immediate results with minimal recovery time.

Microdermabrasion

Microdermabrasion exfoliates the skin to treat light scarring and discoloration. It removes dead skin cells, promoting new skin growth. The result is a brighter and more even skin tone.

Laser Skin Resurfacing

Laser skin resurfacing reduces wrinkles, scars, and blemishes. It uses focused light beams to remove skin layers with precision. The procedure stimulates collagen production for firmer skin.

How much do the procedures cost?

What is the recovery process like?

Are there risks associated with surgery?

How long will the results last?
